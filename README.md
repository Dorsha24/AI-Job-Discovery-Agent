# AI Job Discovery Agent

An AI-powered career intelligence platform that automatically discovers, analyzes, scores, and tracks relevant

## Problem

The modern job market is increasingly competitive.

Candidates often spend hours every week manually searching job boards, reviewing job descriptions, tracking applications, and researching companies.

This process is repetitive, time-consuming, and often leads to missed opportunities.

## Solution

AI Job Discovery Agent automates the entire job discovery workflow.

The platform continuously collects job opportunities, evaluates them using AI, prioritizes the best matches, enriches them with company intelligence, and tracks the application pipeline through a centralized CRM dashboard.

## Key Benefits

- Reduce manual job searching
- Discover opportunities faster
- Prioritize high-value applications
- Track interviews and offers
- Maintain a structured job search process

## Overview

This project automates the entire job discovery process:

1. Collects jobs from multiple Greenhouse-powered companies
2. Uses OpenAI to evaluate job relevance
3. Scores opportunities based on candidate profile
4. Generates company intelligence summaries
5. Sends daily Telegram notifications
6. Stores results in a Google Sheets CRM
7. Provides an analytics dashboard
